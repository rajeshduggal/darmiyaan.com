The Story

It’s been more than 20 years since Aditi and Akash met in Delhi, dated and lost touch with each other. Now Aditi lives in Toronto and Akash lives Singapore. After reconnecting over the internet one day, they plan to meet in New Delhi – and spend half a day walking down memory lanes.
